Late Night Snack
It’s late. You ate dinner and put the kids to bed 3 hours ago, and now it’s almost bedtime but you’re hungry again. Take a slice of bread Slather it with unsweetened peanut butter Pour a generous quantity of hot smoked paprika OR grind a generous quantity of black pepper onto the peanut butter Enjoy!
