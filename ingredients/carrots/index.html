Tuna Salad
Ingredients: 1 can tuna in water 1 can sardines in olive oil Carrot, diced very small Celery, diced very small One small shallot, diced even smaller Capers in brine, some of which should be coarsely chopped Mayonnaise Dijon mustard Preparation: Put tuna in a mixing bowl, reserving the water Add sardines, reserving the oil Add however much of the other ingredients you’d like. I generally start with: about a third of a cup each celery and carrot About a quarter cup of shallot About three tablespoons chopped capers, and another tablespoon whole A tablespoon each mayonnaise and mustard.
