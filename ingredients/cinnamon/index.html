Applesauce
During apple season our local market sells a wonderful variety of very fresh apples. They also sell “second quality” apples in big boxes at a discount. At this time of year I like to make a bunch of applesauce, and freeze most of it for the winter and spring. Of course I’ve got my weird ideas about how to best make applesauce. I like to add pears because I think it adds a nice slightly-floral element.
